Welcome to MOMS

The Modular Organizational Management System (MOMS) is an integrated platform designed to streamline business operations across multiple functional modulars. Our system provides powerful tools for managing human resources, customer relationships, finance, project management, data analytics, automation, collaboration, compliance, strategy, supply chain, etc. You can easily remove any of the modules and/or add modules not provided yet. MOMS is built to adapt to the unique needs of your organization, ensuring flexibility and scalability. The backend system is built on Django, providing a robust and secure foundation for your business processes, and the frontend is built with Vue, ensuring a modern and responsive user experience.

Key Modules

HR Management

Manage employee data, payroll, attendance, and performance evaluations.

CRM

Track customer interactions, manage leads, and improve customer relationships.

Finance

Handle accounting, budgeting, invoicing, and financial reporting.

Project Management

Plan, execute, and monitor projects with task tracking and resource allocation.

Analytics

Gain insights through data visualization and business intelligence tools.

Automation

Automate routine tasks and workflows to improve efficiency.

Collaboration

Enable team communication and document sharing across departments.

Compliance

Ensure adherence to regulations and internal policies.

Strategy

Develop and track strategic initiatives and business goals.

Supply Chain

Manage procurement, inventory, and supplier relationships.

API Access

MOMS provides a comprehensive RESTful API for integrating with other systems. Access our API documentation at Admin Portal.
